If you run a franchise, payroll prep is probably one of the most tedious tasks on your calendar. Every pay cycle, you pull hours from your POS system, copy them into a spreadsheet, manually calculate overtime, reconcile tips, and double-check everything before exporting to your payroll provider. For a multi-store operator, this process can easily take 60 to 75 minutes — and that's when nothing goes wrong.

We built Visio Payroll Prep to eliminate this bottleneck. It connects directly to your POS, pulls the data automatically, surfaces errors your spreadsheet would miss, and exports a ready-to-upload file for Paychex. What used to take an hour now takes five minutes.

## How it works

The process is three steps.

**Connect your POS.** Visio pulls employee hours and credit card tips from LiveIQ automatically. There's no copy-pasting across monitors, no spreadsheet formulas. You set the week-ending date and payment frequency (weekly or biweekly), and the data is already loaded when you open the tool.

**Review flagged exceptions.** Missed clock-outs, overtime anomalies, and adjustments are surfaced for you. Click any employee for a day-by-day breakdown to verify the details. Instead of hunting through raw POS data, you see exactly which days have gaps and can resolve them before exporting.

**Export to Paychex.** One click generates a Paychex-ready CSV. Enter cash tips, confirm totals, done. No reformatting, no intermediate spreadsheets. Upload the file to your Paychex portal and you're finished.

## Why it matters

**Save 60 minutes every pay cycle.** Payroll prep drops from 60–75 minutes to 5–10 minutes per cycle. For a 3-store operator running biweekly payroll, that's 26 hours recovered per year — time you can spend on operations instead of spreadsheets.

**Catch errors your spreadsheet hides.** One franchisee discovered he was overpaying $40 per cycle due to doubled credit card tips — a formula error his spreadsheet had been making for months. Visio surfaces these discrepancies automatically so you fix them before they cost money.

**Eliminate manual data entry.** Hours and tips flow directly from your POS into Visio. No more pulling data from LiveIQ across three monitors, no more consolidating across stores in Excel. The data is already there when you open the tool.

**Get an instant audit trail.** Click any employee to see their day-by-day hours in a list or calendar view. When something doesn't look right, you can verify it in seconds — not by digging through old files.

## Frequently asked questions

**How does Visio pull data from my POS?** Visio connects to LiveIQ and pulls employee hours and credit card tips automatically every pay period. You set the week-ending date and frequency, and the data is already loaded when you open the tool.

**What if my spreadsheet already works fine?** One franchisee thought the same — then Visio found formula errors his spreadsheet had been making for months, including a $40-per-cycle overpayment from doubled credit card tips. Visio doesn't just save time; it audits your existing process.

**Does it handle employees who forget to clock out?** Yes. Missed clock-outs and unusual hours trigger an adjustments indicator on the main payroll table. You can click into any flagged employee to see which days have gaps, then resolve them before exporting.

**What about cash tips?** Cash tips are entered manually in Visio — the system provides an editable field per employee. Credit card tips are pulled automatically from LiveIQ.

**Does it export directly to Paychex?** Yes. Click "Export Paychex CSV" and Visio generates a file formatted exactly for Paychex import. Upload the CSV to your Paychex portal and you're done.
